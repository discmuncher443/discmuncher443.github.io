Our Story


Flying Dutchman Athletics was inspired by the spirit of a pirate - the freedom, the courage, and the grit to carve your own path. I saw that same fire in running: the discipline to show up, the drive to push limits, and the thrill of moving completely free.

Whether you're an elite athlete, a beginner finding your stride, or a young runner dreaming of competing in. college - we're here for you. We build training that meets you where you are and pushes you toward who you want to become.

Here, hard work and science drive everything. No shortcuts. No fear. Just honest miles and the freedom you earch with every step.

Run free. Work hard. Become unstoppable.
